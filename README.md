# YouTube-Sales-Campaign-Optimization-Top-Channel-Recommendation-for-Maximum-ROI

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project analyzes the **ROI (Return on Investment)** of influencer marketing campaigns using Excel and SQL-based calculations.

The goal is to identify **which influencer/channel delivers the highest profit** based on views, conversion rate, product cost, and campaign investment.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

* Evaluate influencer performance
* Estimate potential product sales
* Calculate expected revenue
* Identify the most profitable marketing channel
* Optimize campaign investment decisions

---

## 📌 Input Assumptions

* 📈 Conversion Rate: **2% (0.02)**
* 💰 Product Cost: **$5 per unit**
* 📢 Campaign Cost: **$50,000**

---

## 📊 Channels Analyzed

| Channel           | Avg Views | Potential Sales | Revenue    | Net Profit        |
| ----------------- | --------- | --------------- | ---------- | ----------------- |
| NoCopyrightSounds | 6.9M      | 138,400         | $692,000   | $642,000          |
| DanTDM            | 5.34M     | 106,800         | $534,000   | $484,000          |
| Dan Rhodes        | 11.15M    | 223,000         | $1,115,000 | **$1,065,000 🔥** |

---

## 🧠 Key Calculations

### 🔹 Potential Sales

👉 `Views × Conversion Rate`

### 🔹 Revenue

👉 `Potential Sales × Product Price`

### 🔹 Net Profit

👉 `Revenue – Campaign Cost`

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 🔥 1. Dan Rhodes Dominates Performance

* Highest views: **11.15M**
* Highest revenue: **$1.1M+**
* Highest profit: **$1.06M**

📌 **Insight:**
Dan Rhodes delivers **~2x profit** compared to other influencers

---

### 📊 2. Strong ROI Across All Channels

* All influencers generate **positive net profit**
* Even lowest performer still yields **$484K profit**

📌 **Insight:**
Campaign model is **highly scalable and profitable**

---

### ⚖️ 3. Performance Directly Linked to Reach

* Higher views → higher sales → higher profit

📌 **Insight:**
➡️ **Audience size is the primary revenue driver**

---

### 💡 4. Conversion Rate Leverage

* Even small **2% conversion** generates massive revenue

📌 **Insight:**
➡️ Increasing conversion to **3–4%** could double profit

---

## 🏆 Final Recommendation

👉 **Invest in Dan Rhodes for maximum ROI**

### Why?

* Highest audience reach
* Highest conversion impact
* Maximum profit generation

📌 **Strategic Decision:**
Allocate majority of marketing budget to **top-performing influencers**
